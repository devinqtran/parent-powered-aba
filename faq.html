Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our ABA training courses and family support services.

Getting Started

What is Parent Powered ABA and how is it different from traditional ABA therapy? ▼

Parent Powered ABA is a family-centered approach that teaches parents professional ABA strategies to use at home. Unlike traditional ABA therapy where therapists work directly with your child for hours each week, we empower parents with the same tools and techniques professionals use.

This approach:

  • Reduces dependence on outside services
  • Strengthens family bonds
  • Provides more cost-effective support
  • Gives parents confidence and skills for long-term success
Do I need any prior experience with ABA to take your courses? ▼

No prior experience is required! Our courses are designed for parents with no background in ABA therapy. We start with fundamental concepts and build up to more advanced strategies, ensuring every parent can follow along and succeed.

Our curriculum is beginner-friendly while still providing comprehensive, professional-level training.

Is this suitable for children of all ages and autism levels? ▼

Our strategies are adaptable for children across the autism spectrum and various age groups. The ABA principles we teach are foundational and can be modified based on your child's specific needs, abilities, and developmental level.

During consultations, we help you customize the approaches for your unique family situation.

Course Information

What's included in the 6-unit ABA course curriculum? ▼

Our comprehensive curriculum covers:

  • Unit 1: About ABA and Autism
  • Unit 2: Functional Communication Training
  • Unit 3: Reinforcement Strategies and Motivation
  • Unit 4: Teaching Communication and Social Skills
  • Unit 5: Managing Challenging Behaviors
  • Unit 6: Independence and Lifelong Skills

Each unit includes video lessons, practical exercises, downloadable resources, and real-world examples.

How long does it take to complete the course? ▼

The course is self-paced, allowing you to learn at your own speed. Most parents complete the full curriculum in 6-8 weeks, spending about 2-3 hours per week on course materials and practice.

You have lifetime access to all course materials, so you can revisit content anytime and progress at your own pace.

Do I get a certificate upon completion? ▼

Yes! Upon successful completion of all six units and assessments, you'll receive a Certificate of Completion from Parent Powered ABA, demonstrating your knowledge of fundamental ABA strategies for family use.

Support & Consultations

What can I expect from a personalized consultation? ▼

Our one-on-one consultations provide personalized guidance tailored to your family's specific needs. During a consultation, we:

  • Assess your current situation and challenges
  • Develop customized strategies for your child
  • Create action plans for immediate implementation
  • Provide ongoing support and follow-up recommendations

Sessions typically last 60-90 minutes and can be conducted via video call.

How do the monthly support meetings work? ▼

Our virtual monthly support meetings bring together parents from our community to:

  • Share experiences and challenges
  • Learn from each other's successes
  • Get expert guidance on common issues
  • Build connections with other families

Meetings are held via video conference and typically last 60 minutes. All participants respect confidentiality and create a supportive environment.

Professional Considerations

Should I still work with other professionals while taking your course? ▼

Absolutely! Our services are educational and designed to complement, not replace, professional medical care and therapy services. We encourage parents to work with their child's healthcare team, including pediatricians, behavioral therapists, and other specialists.

Our training helps you be a more informed and effective advocate for your child across all settings.

Are your instructors qualified ABA professionals? ▼

Yes! Our founder, Marieanne Paniagua, is a Board Certified Behavioral Technician with hands-on experience in ABA therapy settings. Our curriculum is developed by professionals with real-world experience in Applied Behavior Analysis.
